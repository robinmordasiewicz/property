Before tackling the vast potential of the 5G edge, mobile network operators (MNOs) need to have an edge strategy that is tailored to their network, their business objectives, and their customers to ensure success upon execution. Every edge strategy is unique and dependent on where you are in your digital transformation journey. Harnessing the power of telco and IT cloud convergence provides increased business agility, enabling real-time network monetization while reducing your security risk.

How devices and applications map to the edge and key considerations for your digital transformation.
